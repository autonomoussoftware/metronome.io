Now is the time to expand and explore with Metronome.

Metronome is a community-driven and community-defined cryptocurrency that will live across multiple blockchains. Want to strengthen your local Metronome community, or build a new one?

Below are resources to build up Metronome meetups, or make existing ones even stronger.
