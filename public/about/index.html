About Metronome.

Metronome was engineered to exceed the high standards of the community. In developing an enduring cryptocurrency, three core design principles build Metronome’s foundation:

Self-Governance

No outside influences. Ever.

MORE INFO

Reliability

The highest level of issuance predictability.

SELF-GOVERNANCE

No Outside Influences. Ever.

With no undue influence from founders after initial launch, Metronome’s founders transitioned to community members, and each MET owner chooses for themselves where they hold their MET.

LEARN MORE »

RELIABILITY

The Highest Level of Issuance Predictability.

With a highly predictable issuance and supply, community members, individuals, and groups can calculate the supply of MET at any date in the future with a high degree of confidence.

MORE ABOUT TOKEN SUPPLY »

PORTABILTY

Now moving freely across blockchains.

Portability frees Metronome from the fate of any one chain. Select the chain that suits your requirements for management and security, or even upgrade the MET contract if needed.
