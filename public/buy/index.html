Buy MET with ease.

Via the Auction Contract or Autonomous Converter Contract..

BUY NOW »

Community members can purchase Metronome without relying on third party exchanges.
There are two ways to purchase:

01.
Auction Contract

Starting at midnight UTC every day, the Auction Contract autonomously mints and auctions 2,880 MET to the public. It uses a descending price auction, with its starting price set at 2x the price auction closing price. The proceeds from these auction stay within the Metronome ecosystem. (More details).

How to buy MET via Auction:

Once the Daily Supply Lot starts, purchasers can wait until the price is what they deem fair, then purchase. They can do this via Metamask or the contract address on the website or via the Metronome wallet itself.

BUY MET »

02.
Autonomous Converter Contract

Community members are able to sell MET or a chain’s underlying token to the Autonomous Converter Contract at a price determined by the current contents of the contract itself. (How the conversion price is determined).

How to buy MET via Autonomous Converter:

The Metronome community can interact with the Autnomous Converter Contract through the Metronome Wallet. The ‘Converter’ tab of the wallet shows the current price, the current contents, and has a ‘Buy Metronome’ button, allowing users to initiate selling an underlying token for MET.

03.
Third Party Exchanges

MET can also be purchased from third party exchanges that have elected to support Metronome..
