Buy MET via the Auction Contract

Every day, the Auction Contract autonomously mints and auctions 2,880 MET to the public. It uses a descending price auction, with its starting price set at 2x the price auction closing price. The proceeds from these auctions stay within the Metronome ecosystem. Once the Daily Supply Lot starts, purchase at the price you deem fair via Metamask or the contract address on the website or the Metronome wallet.

Convert ETH to MET Autonomously

Sell MET or a chain’s underlying token to the Autonomous Converter Contract at a price determined by the current contents of the contract itself. Interact with the Autnomous Converter Contract through the Metronome Wallet. The Converter tab displays the current price, current contents, and Buy button, allowing users to initiate selling an underlying token for MET.
